Project Portfolio


The ephemeral cues of conversation are captured and reflected in the Conversation Clock. This simple social mirror can have a powerful effect on interaction.


Anonymous feedback added to the social mirror creates a new back-channel in face to face conversation. The feedback enables participants to assert themselves in conversation without breaking the rhythm and flow.


The content of a conversation is key to comprehension. Clusters captures conversation context, classifies the content, and coaxes human contribution for corrections.


Isochords demonstrates musical structure by de-emphasizing the physics of the sound wave. Patterns emerge from chords and changes that become both apparent aurally and visually.


A physical avatar for the ephemeral person, Ginger allows direct participation in a cafe space from the comfort of home.


Portal Frame was an early attempt to capture the every day activities that don’t necessitate a phone call or active engagement.


This visualization demonstrates the vibrancy of an active online community, forum, or newsgroup.
